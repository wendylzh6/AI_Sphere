Methodology

Discovery & Selection

Starting from seed accounts (OpenAI, Anthropic, DeepMind, top researchers), we crawl who they follow to find AI voices. If multiple trusted sources follow someone, they matter. The shortlist is then scored by:

Score = log10(followers) x seed_connections

Log scale prevents mega-accounts from dominating. Minimum 1K followers, AI keywords in bio, blocklist for general media.
